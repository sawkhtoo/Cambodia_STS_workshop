Workshop FAQs – Phnom Penh (30–31 October 2025)

Home | FAQs | Contact

General Information

Q: When and where will the workshop take place?

A: The workshop will be held on 30–31 October 2025 in Phnom Penh, Cambodia. Participants are expected to arrive on 29 October and depart on 1 November.

Q: What is the format of the workshop?

A: The event will be conducted onsite and will include technical presentations and working sessions.

Registration

Q: Do I need to register?

A: Yes. All participants must register in advance. A registration link will be shared with confirmed participants.

Travel and Sponsorship

Q: What are my travel entitlements?

A: Sponsored participants are entitled to:

  • DSA: USD 157 per night, payable for 3 nights (29–31 October). DSA is not payable for the departure day (1 November).
  • Travel Advance: ~75% of DSA will be issued prior to departure.
  • Terminal Expenses (TE): USD 252, payable upon mission completion.
  • Remaining DSA Balance: Paid after submission of required travel documentation (boarding passes, tickets, etc.).

Q: In what currency will payments be made?

A: All UN payments are made in USD. The UN does not adjust for any loss or gain from local bank conversions.

Q: How will I receive my DSA and TE?

A: Participants must inform the UN in advance of their preferred payment method and submit the UN standard bank information form. Payments can be made via direct transfer (if your account accepts UN payments). If not, funds may be collected in USD through the UNDP Office in Cambodia.

Accommodation

Q: Will the UN book my hotel?

A: No. Participants must book their own accommodation. The DSA covers hotel, meals, and incidental expenses.

Transportation

Q: Will airport transfers be arranged?

A: No. Participants are responsible for their own airport–hotel–venue transport. TE is provided to cover these costs.

Entry Visas

Q: Do I need a visa for Cambodia?

A: Yes, if applicable. If needed, a support letter can be provided by the organizers.

Workshop Practicalities

Q: What language will the workshop be conducted in?

A: The workshop will be in English, with interpretation provided.

Q: What is the dress code?

A: Business casual.

Q: Are meals included?

A: Coffee breaks and lunches will be provided at the venue. Other meals are at participants’ expense (covered by DSA).

Q: What expenses are not covered?

A: The UN does not cover health/travel insurance, medical costs, personal expenses, or compensation for property loss/damage.

Local Information

Q: What currency is used in Cambodia?

A: Cambodian Riel (KHR) is the official currency, but USD is widely accepted.

Q: What is the local time zone?

A: Phnom Penh is GMT+7.

Q: What electrical plugs are used?

A: Cambodia uses types A, C, and G. Bring an adapter if needed.

Q: What is the weather like in late October?

A: Warm and humid (25–30°C). Light clothing and an umbrella recommended.
